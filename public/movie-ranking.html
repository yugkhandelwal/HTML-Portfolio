Best Movies and TV Shows according to Me

Movies

1. Shawshank redemption(1994)

A compelling drama about the enduring friendship between two imprisoned men, Andy Dufresne and Ellis "Red" Redding, as they navigate the challenges of life in Shawshank State Penitentiary and find hope amidst despair.

2. spirited away(2001)

An enchanting animated fantasy by Hayao Miyazaki, following a young girl named Chihiro who stumbles into a mystical and surreal world of spirits and gods, where she must find a way to save her parents and return to the human world.

3. Spider Man : far from home (2019)

A thrilling superhero adventure featuring Peter Parker as Spider-Man, who embarks on a school trip to Europe. He faces new threats and forms an alliance with the mysterious Mysterio while grappling with the loss of his mentor, Tony Stark.

TV Shows

1. Stranger Things(2016-)

A nostalgic sci-fi horror series set in the 1980s, revolving around a group of kids in the small town of Hawkins, Indiana. They encounter secret government experiments, supernatural forces, and a girl with psychokinetic abilities as they search for their missing friend.

2. Sherlock Holmes(2010-2017)

A modern adaptation of Arthur Conan Doyle's classic detective stories, starring Benedict Cumberbatch as Sherlock Holmes and Martin Freeman as Dr. John Watson. Set in contemporary London, the series follows the brilliant detective as he solves complex cases with his sharp intellect and unique investigative methods.

3. Money Heist(2017-2021)

A gripping Spanish crime drama about an elaborate heist on the Royal Mint of Spain, masterminded by the enigmatic "Professor." The story follows the robbers' complex plan, intense negotiations with the police, and the personal dramas that unfold inside and outside the Mint.
